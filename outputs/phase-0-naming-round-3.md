# Phase 0 Naming Round 3: Cup, Seer, Glory, Vision

## Goal

Explore names beyond the sky direction and beyond direct Oracle names.

This round focuses on:
- Seer
- Glory
- Vision
- Trophy
- Gazer
- Caster
- Compass
- Fun, mystical sports language

## Best New Candidate

Note:
- **MatchSeer** has been selected as the MVP app name.
- The names below remain useful for feature names, campaigns, or future modes.

### The Cup Seer

Why it works:
- It has the oracle vibe without using “Oracle.”
- It is clearer and more charming than “Cup Forecaster.”
- It feels World Cup-specific.
- It can become a character or voice inside the app.
- It does not sound like a sportsbook.

Preliminary checks:
- `thecupseer.com`: not found
- `thecupseer.app`: not found
- `thecupseer.ai`: not found
- `cupseer.com`: registered

Recommendation:
- Best World Cup-specific challenger to **MatchSeer**.
- Use **The Cup Seer** as the phrase, not `CupSeer`.

## Strong Existing Candidate

### MatchSeer

Why it still works:
- Best long-term name.
- Works for World Cup now and NFL Fantasy later.
- Shorter and more flexible than The Cup Seer.
- Preliminary checks from Round 2 looked clean.

Recommendation:
- Keep as the strongest platform name.

## Fun But Riskier Candidates

### The Game Oracle

Why it works:
- Broad and memorable.
- Works beyond World Cup and can stretch into NFL Fantasy.
- Has a clear AI/prophecy feeling.
- Sounds more polished than `GameOracle`.

Preliminary checks:
- `gameoracle.com`: registered
- `thegameoracle.com`: not found
- `gameoracle.app`: not found
- `thegameoracle.app`: timed out during check
- `gameoracle.ai`: not found
- `thegameoracle.ai`: timed out during check

Concern:
- Search results lean toward game theory and video game language.
- “Game” is less football-specific than match, cup, or pitch.
- Since `gameoracle.com` appears registered, users may land elsewhere if they drop “The.”
- “Oracle” still carries trademark and search noise.

Recommendation:
- Good contender if we want a broader sports/AI identity.
- Prefer **The Game Oracle** over `GameOracle`.
- Slightly weaker than **MatchSeer** because it is broader and noisier.

## Recommendation

If we want the app to become a broader sports forecasting product:
- Use **MatchSeer**.

If we want the World Cup launch to feel more magical and event-specific:
- Use **The Cup Seer**.

If we want the brand to feel like an AI sports oracle across many sports:
- Consider **The Game Oracle**, but only after trademark and domain checkout.

Potential product structure:
- App name: **MatchSeer**
- World Cup mode: **The Cup Seer**
- Feature voice: **The Oracle**
- Share card label: “Seen by MatchSeer”
