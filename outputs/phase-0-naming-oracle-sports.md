# Phase 0 Naming Round 2: Oracle And Sports Direction

## Goal

Move away from the sky/UFO flavor and explore names that feel more like:
- A sports oracle
- A playful match forecaster
- A smart companion for World Cup and future NFL Fantasy
- Fun, not too serious
- Non-betting

## Naming Recommendation

Top recommendation: **MatchSeer**

Status:
- Confirmed as the MVP app name.

Why:
- It has the oracle/forecast feeling without using the word “Oracle.”
- It is sports-friendly and easy to understand.
- It can stretch from World Cup matches to NFL matchups.
- It feels modern and a little mystical without sounding like gambling.
- Preliminary checks looked clean.

Strong alternate: **The Cup Oracle**

Why:
- It sounds more memorable than `CupOracle`.
- It directly communicates the World Cup forecast idea.
- It gives the product a fun oracle character from day one.
- It is better as a phrase than as a compound word.

Caution:
- The word “Oracle” creates search and trademark noise.
- Search results can drift toward Oracle Team USA and the America’s Cup.
- It is less flexible for NFL Fantasy later unless we treat it as a World Cup product name.

## Why Not Use “Oracle” Directly

The word “Oracle” has the right energy, but it has drawbacks:
- Many direct domain names are already registered.
- It can create search and trademark noise because of Oracle Corporation.
- It may feel heavier or more corporate than the playful product we want.

Better direction:
- Use “seer,” “sage,” or an in-app oracle character.
- Example: app name **MatchSeer**, feature voice **The Match Oracle**.
